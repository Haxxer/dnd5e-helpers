Class: WildMagicSurge

WildMagicSurge()

Implements stock surge rules, and two homebrew variants. "More Surges" will surge if the d20 is less than or equal to the spell level cast. "Volatile Surges" will add a d4 to the spell level if the sorcerer has their Tides feature expended.
